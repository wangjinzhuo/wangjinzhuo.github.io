About Me

I am a Ph.D Candidate at Peking University, working with Prof. Wen Gao and Prof. Wenmin Wang. Prior to that, I obtained B.S. degree at Peking University under the supervision of Prof. Yanchun Sun.

 

My research interest broadly lies in machine learning and computer vision. My research aims to tackle challenging problems, and develop "light-and-sweet" algorithms: (i) light: conceptually simple in methodology and easy to implement in practice, (ii) sweet: having clear intuitions or non-trivial theoretical guarantees.
